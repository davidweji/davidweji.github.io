Field Overview

a hardware engineer is responsible for designing, developing, and testing the physical components of computer systems and other electronic devices.
This may include computer chips, circuit boards, systems for cooling and power management, and other components.
The goal of a hardware engineer is to create products that are efficient, reliable, and capable of meeting the needs of consumers and businesses.
This requires a strong understanding of electrical engineering principles, as well as the ability to work with a wide range of tools and technologies.
Hardware engineers often collaborate with software engineers to ensure that the hardware and software components of a device work together seamlessly.

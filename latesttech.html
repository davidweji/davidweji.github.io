Quantum Computing

Quantum Computing is a way of doing calculations that is different from regular computers. Instead of using regular bits like normal computers, quantum computers use things called qubits which can be both 0 and 1 at the same time.
This means quantum computers can solve certain types of problems much faster. In recent years, many big companies like IBM, Google, Microsoft, and Intel have become interested in building quantum computers and making tools
that use quantum computing. They are trying to be the first to use this new technology in many different industries like finance, healthcare, and energy.
